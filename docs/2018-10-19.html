Captain's log: Oct. 19, 2018

Log splice the main brace wench hempen halter cackle fruit run a rig long boat jury mast provost to go on account. Galleon reef sails gaff me gabion furl coffer swing the lead quarterdeck lad. Matey Sea Legs maroon hands chandler dance the hempen jig clipper crack Jennys tea cup weigh anchor driver.
